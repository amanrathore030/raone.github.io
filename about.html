About Us

Building a New Enterprising World

  • Having started as a Professional Contracting Company in Delhi in 1994, we have cemented a gratifying 
    repute in construction industry over these years.
  • Gradually we have built on our core strengths, namely, Quality Construction, State-of-the-art technology 
    and skilled work force to promote our very own buildings and deliver high standard excellence in this field.
  • It is this inspiration that has motivated everyone of us at SH Associates, while we stand committed in 
    transcending our clients' expectation.
  • Since our incorporation, we have acquired expertise in construction contracts, and have also recently 
    forayed into Urban Infrastructure projects. We commenced our construction projects by undertaking two 
    contracts of INR 0.3 million each in 1994 and presently have a prequalification capacity to quote for 
    contracts over INR 300 million.

     

Pre-Construction :

  • The Stepping Stone To Quality Construction
    Our ears attentively listen to our clients requirement and expectation, Our mind works out on the feasibility of 
    the project, the architectural style, the layout design plan, the financial costing, the building mechanics including 
    number of floors, kind of fixtures and fittings and of course, the tentative time of project completion.
  • Design & Build : Symbiosis Between Dream And Reality
  • Good understanding / rapport between the design team and the engineers on-site
  •  Better understanding of the drawings and the technicalities 
  • Proper coordination between the site and management
  • Immediate response possible from both, in case of any changes or emergency
  • Effective time management as clients need to deal with only one team.

Safety Measures & Environment

  • Protecting People, Saving Lives & protecting Environment
  • A clean site is a safe site. It reduces the risk of injury. Our leadership in safety stands tall. We've 
    pioneered the use of worker safety plan. The rigorous safety controls like wearing head gears make 
    every individual to play an active role in risk prevention. The sourcing of alcohol free onsite workers 
    improve relationships and job site efficiency. The results have been so effective, we have passed six 
    long accident free years.
  • We at SH Associates have concern for the environment and pollution control and take every step 
    possible to reduce pollution and save energy.
  • As an organization we feel responsible towards Our Clients, Our Team and above all the Human Race. 
    This impels us to develop only GREEN BUILDINGS that help live in a healthy and a happy environment 
    at a reasonable cost. These sustainable developments use far less natural resources and energy in the 
    process they are built and used.

Our Esteemed Associates

  • Joseph Stein, (10 April 1912 –6 October 2001) was an American architect and a major figure in the 
    establishment of a regional modern architecture in the San Francisco Bay area in the 1940s and 1950s 
    during the early days of the environmental design movement. 
    In 1952 he moved to India. He is noted for designing several important buildings in India, most notably in 
    Lodhi Estate in Central Delhi, nicknamed "Steinabad" after him, and where today the 'Joseph Stein Lane', 
    is the only road in Delhi named after an architect. The Government of India awarded him the fourth highes 
    civilian award of Padma Shriin 1992.
  • Fariborz Sahba, a Canadian citizen, was born in 1948 in Iran. He received a master's degree in architecture
    in 1972 from the Faculty of Fine Arts at Tehran University. In Iran, Mr.Sahba was involved in the design 
    of a wide range of prestigious buildings. In 1976, the international governing body of 
    the Bahá'ícommunity selected Mr.Sahbato design the Bahá'íHouse of Worship for the Indian subcontinent in New Delhi, India. This project, on which he worked for10 years as the architect and project manager, was described by Canadianarchitect Arthur Erickson as "one of the most remarkable achievements of our time, proving that the drive
    and vision of spirit can achieve miracles." With over 3.5 million visitors a year, this building, commonly 
    known as the "Lotus of Bahapur," is one of the most visited sites in the world.
  • The Shapoorji Pallonji Group (SP Group) is an Indian business conglomerate in India with interests in 
    construction, real estate, textiles, engineering goods, home appliances, shipping, publications, power, and 
    biotechnology.
